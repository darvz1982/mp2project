About

Join us at La Quinta Inn & Suites by Wyndham Madera, perfectly positioned off Highway-99 for easy access to all that the area has to offer. Explore nearby attractions like the Madera Wine Trail, ApCal, and Madera Speedway. If you're flying in our out of the area, Fresno Yosemite International Airport (FAT) is less than 30 miles from our hotel. While staying with us, you'll love amenities like free breakfast, an outdoor pool, and pet-friendly rooms.
